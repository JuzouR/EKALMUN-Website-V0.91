About the Conference

Welcome to the Ertuğrul Kurdoğlu Anatolian High School Model United Nations (EKALMUN). As a prestigious simulation of the United Nations, our conference aims to bring together passionate students to debate global issues, foster international cooperation, and develop diplomatic solutions to complex problems. EKALMUN provides a unique platform for delegates to step into the shoes of world leaders, enhancing their public speaking, critical thinking, and negotiation skills.

This year, we are committed to hosting a conference that not only simulates the procedural aspects of the UN but also emphasizes the spirit of empathy and global citizenship. Our academic team has meticulously prepared agendas that challenge delegates to think outside the box and address the most pressing issues of our time, from international security crises to economic instability and human rights violations.

Frequently Asked Questions

Where will the conference take place?

The conference will be held at Ertuğrul Kurdoğlu Anatolian High School in Çayırova, Kocaeli.

Is there a dress code?

Yes, delegates are expected to adhere to Western Business Attire (WBA) throughout the sessions.

What procedure will be followed for the conference?

Our conference will follow Harvard MUN procedures.
