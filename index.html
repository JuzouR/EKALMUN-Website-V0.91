About the Conference

The Ertuğrul Kurdoğlu Anatolian High School Model United Nations (EKALMUN) is a premier academic simulation designed to bring the complex world of international diplomacy into a student-led environment. Our conference serves as a dynamic platform for motivated individuals to step into the roles of global decision-makers, addressing the world’s most pressing challenges through formal debate and collaboration. By participating in these sessions, delegates move beyond the classroom to develop essential life skills, including persuasive public speaking, strategic negotiation, and deep analytical thinking, all within a professional and supportive atmosphere.

This year, our focus remains on providing a high-quality experience that encourages students to find practical solutions to real-world problems. We strive to create an immersive environment where delegates are challenged to think critically and work together to draft resolutions that reflect the true spirit of international cooperation. Each session is an opportunity for participants to test their diplomatic abilities and gain a deeper understanding of the mechanisms that govern our global society, ensuring a transformative journey for every attendee.

Our vision for EKALMUN is to inspire a new generation of empathetic and responsible global citizens. We believe that true leadership is built on a foundation of mutual respect and the ability to understand diverse perspectives. Beyond the rules of procedure, we aim to foster a lasting community of young leaders who are committed to making a positive impact on the world. Our ultimate goal is to ensure that every student leaves this conference with the confidence and the vision needed to contribute to a more peaceful, just, and equitable global future.

Frequently Asked Questions

Where will the conference take place?

The conference will be held at Ertuğrul Kurdoğlu Anatolian High School in Çayırova, Kocaeli.
